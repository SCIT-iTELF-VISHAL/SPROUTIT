About Sprout'IT

About SCIT

Symbiosis Centre for Information Technology, a constituent of Symbiosis International University has been a pioneer and a leader in imparting education in the niche area of Information Technology Business Management in India for more than two decades. SCIT nurture genuine IT talent to become future leaders in the Information Technology arena.

About iTELF

iTELF is the Entrepreneurship Cell of SCIT, dedicated to nurturing entrepreneurial spirit and transforming aspiring minds into victorious leaders of tomorrow. We bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world business challenges.

Events & Activities

Biznovator

“Innovation Seeds Tomorrow.”

Biznovator is Symcredia’s stage for entrepreneurial originality and thoughtful disruption. It invites the curious, the creative, and the committed to present ventures that reflect not just innovation, but purpose and possibility. Here, business ideas are cultivated in an environment that values insight as much as impact. Participants engage with mentors, industry minds, and peers to evolve their ideas into credible, investor-ready ventures. Biznovator isn’t about spectacle — it’s about substance. It’s where tomorrow’s ventures take thoughtful shape, ready to inspire and influence.

Founder’s Den

“Step Into the Den, Emerge a Founder.”

Founder’s Den is your curtain-raiser into the entrepreneurial symphony of Symcredia. This is Symcredia’s powerhouse of raw ambition and strategic matchmaking. Here, aspiring founders walk in with an idea and walk out with a mission, a team, and a sharpened business instinct. The raw notes of an idea are tuned into harmony through collaboration, critique, and co-creation. Whether you’re pitching or partnering, the Den is where founders are born and ventures begin their first act.
