iTELF is the Entrepreneurship Cell of SCIT, dedicated to nurturing entrepreneurial spirit and transforming aspiring minds into victorious leaders of tomorrow. We bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world business challenges.

Events & Activities

AD MAD

Advertising Campaign Competition – Showcase your creative marketing skills and develop innovative conceptual advertisement scripts and ideas that captivate audiences.

Rural Transformation Challenge

Combine and plan innovative solutions using various rural products for effective rural-urban transformation and sustainable development.

Documentary & Movie Exhibits

Curated showcases featuring successful entrepreneurs and startups. Get inspired, engage in meaningful discussions, and expand your network.

Turnaround Business Challenge

Tackle real-world failed business problems, develop comprehensive turnaround plans, and demonstrate strategic thinking and execution skills.

Thinking Cap Competition

Teams get a creative "thinking cap" perspective to design imaginative marketing strategies for fictional companies, inspired by classic Stanford methodologies.
